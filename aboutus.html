About Us

Hello everyone, I Think I'm a Nerd welcomes you to our brand. We are a group of friends and nerds who work on making a plethora of different types of videos, including gaming videos, video essays, skits, and more. We hope you enjoy all of our content and we would love any and all feedback you have to give.
